Travel to NuConference:

Lodging:

The Hilton Monona Terrace

The hotel is connected to Monona Terrace Community and Convention Center via a skywalk. It's within blocks of State Street and the WI State Capitol, and the University of Wisconsin Madison campus is a mile away. A free shuttle will take you to and from Dane County Airport and local attractions. Enjoy our 24-hour market and indoor pool.

Conference Center:

Monona Terrace Conference Center

The Monona Terrace® Community & Convention Center is a meeting venue you can't find anywhere else. This spectacular 250,000 square-foot, multi-level facility features public promenades, meeting rooms with striking lake views and the attached Hilton Madison Monona Terrace. Gracing the rooftop is a 68,000 square-foot garden that may be reserved for events. And the location? Just two blocks from the Wisconsin State Capitol. On the shores of Lake Monona. It doesn't get any better.

Air Transport:

Dane County Regional Airport

Dane County Regional Airport (MSN) now offers nonstop flights to 19 destinations: Atlanta, Charlotte, Chicago, Dallas-Fort Worth, Denver, Detroit, Fort Myers, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Minneapolis, New York, Newark, Orlando, Philadelphia, Phoenix, Salt Lake City, San Francisco, Tampa and Washington D.C.
